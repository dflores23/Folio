Lets not talk about my journey

I should start with an admission: I’m not a storyteller.

But I want to share a sliver of my story (I’ll keep it brief) because we are shaped by our experiences.

At the age of 15, I left Mexico (and my family) to finish high school in the US.

I learned English, graduated, and started a successful career in restaurant management.

From 2005-2021, I managed high performing restaurants, recruited and trained large teams, and shaped a customer experience that we were proud of. Restaurant work is grueling, and it taught me two main things: the importance of your team and the opportunity for efficiency.

In January 2022, I finished General Assembly’s Software Engineering Immersive and rounded out about two years of independently learning to code.

I have experience in HTML, JavaScript, MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node), Css, Sass, Bootstrap, jQuery, SQL and Ruby on Rails, and I’m ready to take my grit and curiosity into my second career as a Software Developer.

Gracias,

David
